Sexpedition rules
1. The strength of the heroine is the number of her promotions.
2. The strength of a superheroine is the number of her stars.
3. Sexpedition consists of a chain of battles that follow each other.
4. Each battle requires a certain amount of energy.
5. Each battle has its own limitations on the available heroines.
6. The reward for fighting is chaos chests and coins of chaos. For coins of chaos, you can buy additional chaos chests in the store.
7. Opening a chaos chest gives points for the rating of the sexpedition, can give points for energy and superheroes.
8. Those who take the highest places in the ranking will receive unique superheroines.
